How Face Recognition Works

ImageVision utilizes advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to detect and recognize faces in your images. The process works as follows:

  • Image Acquisition: The system captures faces from images using cameras or uploaded files.
  • Face Detection: Using computer vision algorithms, it locates faces within the image.
  • Facial Landmark Identification: The system detects key facial landmarks like eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Feature Extraction: It generates a unique numerical faceprint based on facial features.
  • Face Matching: The generated faceprint is compared with others in the database for identification.
  • Learning and Improvement: The system improves over time, getting smarter with every use.

By automating face recognition, ImageVision saves time and enhances the way you organize and search your event images.
